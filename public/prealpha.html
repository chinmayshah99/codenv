CodEnv Pre-Alpha Usage

For Windows 10 Home Users:

  1. Download and Install DockerTookbox
  2. Download CodEnv!
  3. Download and extract the CodEnv.zip in your Documents Folder
  4. Download and extract the codenv-win32-x64.zip at your place of convienence.
  5. Go to the extracted folder of codenv-win32-x64.zip and open codenv.exe
    6. Note: While running CodEnv the first time, the system might take a while to setup, depending on your internet connection.
    After booting your system, Open Docker Quick Start terminal before openning the CodEnv application (to be fixed in next release). -- Start CodEnv after you can type something on Docker Quick Start terminal.
